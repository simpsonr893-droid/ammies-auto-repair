Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the CrimeMap app.

CrimeMap aggregates data from 2,200+ official police department feeds, FBI Uniform Crime Report (UCR) databases, 911 dispatch logs, and verified community reports. Every incident is cross-referenced against at least two sources before it appears on the crime hotspot map, ensuring accuracy you can rely on.
The safety score for each block is recalculated every hour using a rolling 90-day window of incident data. Major incidents trigger an immediate recalculation. This means your safety rating reflects both long-term trends and very recent events — far more current than any published index.

The route safety planner generates up to five alternative routes between your origin and destination. Each route is scored using real-time crime hotspot map data, time-of-day crime patterns, and lighting/infrastructure data. Routes are color-coded green (safe), yellow (caution), and red (avoid). The algorithm updates in real time as new community safety alerts come in.
