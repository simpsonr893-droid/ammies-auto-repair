Real-Time Crime Alert Subscriptions

Know what's happening in your neighborhood the moment it's reported — not hours later. CrimeMap's real-time crime alerts aggregate data from official police feeds and verified community reports to deliver notifications in under 30 seconds.

CrimeMap is the only personal safety app that simultaneously monitors police CAD feeds, 911 dispatch data, and verified community submissions for every covered area. When an incident is confirmed across two or more sources, an alert fires. This multi-source approach eliminates the false positive rate that plagues single-source apps while maintaining industry-leading speed.

Alert zones are defined by radius (as small as a quarter-mile block radius) and can be set for your home, workplace, your children's school, elderly relatives' addresses — anywhere that matters to you. Free accounts support up to 3 zones. Premium unlocks unlimited zones across unlimited cities.

  • ⚡Sub-30-second delivery — faster than any news app, next-door platform, or police department social feed
  • 🎯Multi-source verification — alerts require confirmation from at least 2 independent sources before firing
  • 🔕Smart alert modes — silent notifications for low-severity; loud interruptions for high-priority events
  • 🗂️Crime type filters — choose which incident types matter: theft, assault, vandalism, carjacking, burglary, and more
  • 🌍Traveling? — set temporary alert zones for hotels, venues, or any destination before you arrive

Route Safety Planner

The safest route isn't always the fastest one — and now you don't have to guess which it is. CrimeMap's route safety planner scores every path between any two points using real-time crime hotspot map data.

Whether you're planning a morning run, navigating an unfamiliar city, or deciding which way to walk your kids to school, the route safety planner gives you data most navigation apps simply don't have. Each route alternative is overlaid with color-coded safety segments — green for safe, yellow for caution, red for high-risk — drawn from the latest block-level crime data.

The planner doesn't just optimize for safety — it offers a spectrum of routes that balance safety and travel time, so you can make the tradeoff that fits your situation. A longer safe route at midnight is often the right call; a slightly lower-scoring route that saves 15 minutes at noon might be perfectly reasonable.

Block-Level Crime Data Export

Raw, structured crime statistics by city and block — exported as CSV, JSON, or GeoJSON. The same block-level crime data that powers CrimeMap's consumer app, available for your analysis workflows.

CrimeMap's data export service gives researchers, analysts, journalists, and enterprise clients direct access to the underlying incident dataset. Every export includes incident type, geocoded location (to 100m grid cell), timestamp, source type (police/community/CAD), severity score, and verification status.

Data can be filtered by city, neighborhood, date range, incident type, and severity threshold. Exports are delivered via SFTP or direct download, with options for scheduled recurring exports (daily, weekly, or monthly) for users who need a continuously updated data feed without API integration.

  • 📁Three export formats — CSV for spreadsheet analysis, JSON for developers, GeoJSON for GIS mapping
  • 🗓️Custom date ranges — export any time window from 24 hours to 5 years of historical data
  • 🔍Granular filtering — filter by city, neighborhood, incident type, severity, and source type
