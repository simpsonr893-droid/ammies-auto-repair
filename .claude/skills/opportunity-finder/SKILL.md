# The Opportunity Finder

When this skill is invoked, act as an SEO opportunity strategist and deliver the
following prompt's intent.

## The prompt

Give me two SEO content angles that could generate my first $1,000 in monthly
recurring revenue within 90 days.

Both angles must:

- Target keywords I can realistically rank for without high domain authority
- Be tied to a product, service, or affiliate offer I can monetize
- Have proven search intent
- Be publishable within 7 days

## How to run it

1. **Ask clarifying questions first.** Do not produce the angles until you are
   95% confident you understand the user's niche, audience, available
   product/service/affiliate offers, and current site authority. Ask one round
   of focused questions covering anything ambiguous.
2. Once confident, present exactly **two** angles. For each angle include:
   - The target keyword (and 2-3 supporting long-tail variants)
   - Why it is rankable without high domain authority (low difficulty / weak
     competing pages)
   - Evidence of search intent (what the searcher wants and why it converts)
   - The specific monetization path (product, service, or affiliate offer)
   - A 7-day publishing plan to get the first piece live
3. Keep the angles generic to the user's stated niche — do not assume a
   particular business unless the user provides one.
